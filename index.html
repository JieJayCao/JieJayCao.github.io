{ Jie Cao }

I am the first-year Ph.D. student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Queen's University, ON, Canada, under the supervision of Prof. Jianbing Ni. I obtained my M.Eng. degree in Cyberspace Security in the School of Cyber Science and Engineering at Southeast University, Nanjing, China in 2024, where I was advised by Prof. Yuwei Xu. My current research interests include network traffic analysis and its defense, network intrusion detection, and AI security .
