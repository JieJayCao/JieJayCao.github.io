{ Jie Cao }

I am the first-year Ph.D. student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Queen's University, ON, Canada, under the supervision of Prof. Jianbing Ni. I obtained my M.Eng. degree in Cyberspace Security in the School of Cyber Science and Engineering at Southeast University, Nanjing, China in 2024, where I was advised by Prof. Yuwei Xu. Prior to that, I received my B.S. degree in Information Security (Specialized Program in Cyberspace Security) from Xi'an University of Posts & Telecommunications, Xi'an, China in 2020, under the guidance of Prof. Chengzhe Lai and Prof. Jingyu Feng. My current research interests include network traffic analysis and its defense, network intrusion detection, and AI security .
